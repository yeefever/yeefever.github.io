Martin Hale received a thank you note on an otherwise unremarkable July Sunday. 
It arrived by post, a pleasingly off-white envelope, with an air of extravagance that implied considerable forethought. His name and return address inscribed in a curvy, almost laviscious manner that suggested a fountain pen budgeted for the bestowal of gratitude. From a glance, the parcel contained no more than 2 pieces of paper; he gingerly flipped it over and ran his thumb over the ornate wax seal. It gave off a rubbery hand-feel and felt cold to touch. Strange, he thought to himself. Quite strange.

Inside was a single cream-colored slip of paper decorated with the same swirly handwriting as the container it arrived in. 
> *Dear Martin,*\
*Thank you again for stepping in when no one else would. I don’t know what we would have done* *without you.* \
*Truly,* \
*—E*

Martin read it through twice then a third time at a slower pace. He did not recognize the initial E nor did he recall stepping in. He shook the envelope and turned it upside down, embarrassed by the hope that he might have missed something. He stood at the kitchen counter for a while, kettle screaming beside him. Eventually the kettle shutoff clicked and Martin, still scrutinizing the letter, poured the water into his cup. Muscle memory, it turned out, still functioned when memory did not.

![Bocchi](/images/bocchi.png)

The following week, another note arrived.

This one caught Martin midweek, slipped through the mail slot sometime between his leaving for work and returning home. He noticed it immediately with his name carved across the front with the same undeserving ceremony. He stood in the entryway, keys in hand, a bit longer than necessary. He considered waiting until he was inside his house to open it. The thought surprised him. He had never been precious about his mail. 

Once safely inside, the seal yielded with the same mellow resistance. The ink was slightly darker this time. 
> *Dear Martin,*\
*Thank you for staying when it would have been easier to leave. We noticed.*\
*—E*
